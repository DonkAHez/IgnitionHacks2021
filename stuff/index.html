UAP - The University Acceptance Predictor




An AI-powered tool to predict your chance of being accepted into a given university program.




What is UAP?

UAP is an AI-driven program that predicts what percentage chance you may have at getting into a given university strictly based on marks. You can get this prediction by submitting your school and course of choice, as well as your grade average from your last year at high school in the form above.
